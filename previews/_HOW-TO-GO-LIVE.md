# Put the previews online — 2 minutes, no code

You said midlandsmade.ie is on Netlify, so you already have a Netlify account. This gets every preview a real shareable link.

## Fastest way (drag & drop — no login-fuss)
1. On a laptop, open **https://app.netlify.com/drop**
2. Drag the WHOLE folder `D:\Midlands_Web\previews` onto the page.
3. Netlify gives you a live URL like `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app`.
4. Your previews are instantly at:
   - `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app/daly/`
   - `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app/nolan-motors/`
   - `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app/evan-longford/`
   - `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app/dunleavy-heating/`
   - `https://silly-name-1234.netlify.app/brendan-norris/`
5. Send me that base URL in chat and I'll drop the real links into every outreach email automatically.

## Nicer way (optional, later)
In Netlify: rename the site to something like `midlands-previews`, then point a
subdomain `previews.midlandsmade.ie` at it (Netlify → Domain settings → add subdomain).
Then links read `previews.midlandsmade.ie/daly/` — looks like your brand. Not needed for
the first sends; the .netlify.app link works perfectly to show a client.
