Good to know

Straight answers
before you visit.

Do you fit the stoves you sell?

Yes. Our OFTEC-registered installers supply, deliver and fit the stove, flue, hearth and surround, so you deal with one team throughout.

What areas do you cover?

We work across Kildare, Laois and the surrounding Midlands. Call with your location and the team will confirm availability.

Can I see working stoves?

Yes. The showroom has live displays so you can compare the look, scale and heat before you decide.

What should I bring to the showroom?

Room measurements, a few photos of the existing fireplace and any ideas you have for the finish are a very useful start.
