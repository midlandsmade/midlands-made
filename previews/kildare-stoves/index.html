Good to know

Stove & fireplace FAQs

Do you fit the stoves you sell?
Yes. We're OFTEC-registered solid-fuel installers, so we supply, deliver and fit your stove, flue, hearth and surround — one team from the showroom floor to the finished fire.
What areas do you cover?
We're based on the Monasterevin Road in Co. Kildare and cover the Midlands and much of the country — we regularly travel further afield for the right job. Tell us where you are and we'll let you know what suits.
What types of stove do you sell?
Wood-burning and multi-fuel room heater stoves, boiler stoves, pellet stoves and electric stoves — plus inset fireplaces, flues, hearths and handmade mantelpieces.
What size stove do I need?
As a rough guide, divide your room's volume in cubic metres by about 14 to get the kW output to aim for. Try our size guide above, then we'll size it properly for your room, chimney and insulation.
Can I see the stoves in person before I buy?
Yes — our Monasterevin showroom has a wide range of stoves and fireplaces on display, so you can see the models, sizes and finishes in person and get honest advice on what suits your room. Call in during opening hours or book a time that suits you.
