What we collect

  • Enquiry details — when you use our contact/enquiry form, we collect your name, the phone number or email you give us, and anything you write in your message.
  • Basic website data — like most websites, our host may log standard technical information (browser type, approximate location, pages visited) to keep the site secure and working well.

How we use it

  • To reply to your enquiry and give you a quote or advice.
  • To arrange a showroom visit, survey, delivery or fitting.
  • To keep our website secure and improve it.

We do not sell your information, and we don't use it for automated decision-making or profiling.

Who we share it with

We only share your information with trusted service providers who help us run the business — for example our website host and the service that delivers our contact-form emails to us. If you go ahead with a purchase, payment is handled by a secure payment provider; we never see or store your full card details. We may also share information where we're legally required to.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and customer information only as long as we need it to help you and to meet our legal and accounting obligations, then we delete it.

Cookies

Essential cookies keep the site working and are always on. Any non-essential cookies (for example analytics) are only used with your consent, and you can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to ask us for a copy of your information, to correct it, to have it deleted, to object to or restrict how we use it, and to receive it in a portable format. To exercise any of these, just contact us. You also have the right to complain to the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie).
